About

The Ashesi E-yearbook is a platform dedicated to help final students and faculty put together their unfogettable memories. It allows people from the community who have signed up to be able to interact with the platform and have acess to multiple yearbooke without necessarily printing them. It is available to users of the site. A team comes together every year to collect all the memories and gather them into one book for you to enjoy the moments you never thought would be captured.
